Booking Details

Once you submit your booking request, you will receive a confirmation with the following details:

  • Booking Reference: A unique code for your booking.
  • Departure City: The city from which your journey will start.
  • Destination City: The city where you will arrive.
  • Travel Date: The date of your journey.
  • Number of Passengers: Total number of passengers.
  • Payment Status: Confirmation of successful payment.

Keep this information safe as you will need it for any future reference or changes to your booking.
